# Privacy Policy for NextGenKeyboard

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]

## 1. Data Collection
NextGenKeyboard respects your privacy. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

### 1.1 Crash Reporting
We use **Firebase Crashlytics** to improve the stability of the app. This service may collect:
- Device type and operating system version
- Stack traces of crashes
- Timestamp of the crash

**You can opt-out of crash reporting in the app Settings.**

### 1.2 Typing Data
All typing data is processed locally on your device. We **do not** transmit your keystrokes, passwords, or personal messages to any external server.

## 2. Permissions
- **Microphone:** Used only for Voice Typing feature. Audio is not stored.
- **Internet:** Used for fetching GIFs (via Giphy) and downloading language dictionaries.

## 3. Third-Party Services
- **Giphy:** Used for GIF search. Please refer to [Giphy's Privacy Policy](https://support.giphy.com/hc/en-us/articles/360032872931-GIPHY-Privacy-Policy).

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [support@nextgenkeyboard.com].
